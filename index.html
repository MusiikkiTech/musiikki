Bookings

Musiikki is proud to host a wide variety of local, regional, and international musical acts! To send a booking request please fill out the form below, or send an email to musiikkibookings@gmail.com. Please include the following information in your request:

  • Recorded examples of your music.
  • A specific requested date, or range of dates.
  • Links to social media (Artist website, Facebook, YouTube, etc.).
  • A stage plot, or list of required inputs.

We typically book 2-3 months in advance, so please plan ahead with your requests. Please allow up to 2 weeks before sending a follow up request. Additional information about preforming at Musiikki can be found here. Thank you for your interest!

Musiikki Open Mic Night

Rules:

  • Respect The Space, Performers, Audience, and Host/Temp.
  • Audience**Be quiet and attentive during performances.
  • Performers**Must stay within their time limits (including set up time and material introductions).
  • Performers**Do not call out audience members, the host will deal with discrepancies.
  • No established full bands or artists that bring large followings.
  • No intoxicated performances please.
​

Note:

You may be asked to leave if you are caught breaking any of these rules.

Fruequently Asked Questions

  • Q. Where do i sign up?
  • A. Online sign up is at 12 p.m. - 2 p.m. on the day of the open mic. Walk-in sign ups are available at musiikki cafe through your bartender from 12 p.m. onward. any online sign up that is not filled by 2 p.m. will automatically become a walk-in time slot.
​
  • Q. How do I sign up online?
  • A. Direct Message through the musiikki Cafe open mic facebook or instagram page.
​
  • Q. How Long is my set
  • A. 15 minutes including set up and tear down. For musicians/duos/trios, generally 3 songs or 2 longer songs. for other performances (comedy, spoken word, poetry, etc.) consider structuring your act around 12 minutes.
​
  • Q. What if i can't fill 15 minutes?
  • A. That is okay! We will have surplus time for overflow performers!
​ Note: As a frequent/returning performer, you may be asked to share some of your time slot with newcomers that are not aware of sign up.
  • Q. Is there house instruments/Microphones?
  • A. Yep! We have a house guitar, house percussion (a Cajon), a bass amp, a guitar amp, and microphones available.
​
  • Q. Can i bring my own mic/instruments/special cables?
  • A. Yes, bringing your own instruments and special cables is encouraged. However, please consider only bringing dynamic microphones.
​
  • Q. Can i set up drums?
  • A. Yep, but please alert the bartender/host in advance if you plan to bring a drum set.
Note: all drum sets must be set up 1 hour before open mic begins, and you will not be able to tear down until open mic has ended. ​ Open Mic Sign Up Slots **Summer Schedule In Bold**
